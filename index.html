Why Donate?

Two-thirds of people in India live in poverty: 68.8% of the Indian population lives on less than $2 a day. Over 30% even have less than $1.25 per day available - they are considered extremely poor.

Donations are helpful to students because they provide access to education, reduce financial burdens, enhance learning opportunities, support extracurricular activities, provide specialized support, create a positive community impact, and promote research and innovation. By donating, individuals and organizations can make a meaningful difference in the lives of students and contribute to the betterment of society as a whole.
